The 2-Week Timeline

We’ve taken our experience with creating engaging brand and narrowed down the most impactful elements of our Designtific Method. We packed these elements into a 2-week timeline that delivers amazing results.

Strategy Workshop Day
Work side-by-side with experts

Your team plus the Sodium Halogen team will share an intense day of workshop exercises to get to know even more about you company.

  • We will learn about the Why, How, and What.
  • We will really get to know your target audience on an individual level.
  • We will talk about your messaging and why you're unique.
