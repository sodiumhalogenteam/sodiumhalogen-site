Strategy

Identify the problem(s)

DC wanted to grow, but their cobbled together system was holding them back

Teachers were frustrated with changes

DC was susceptible to existing learning platforms (1 yr vendor doubled their price)

Customer centered

HS students

Teachers

CTE directors

Insight / action

DC needed their own platform and curriculum

If we can make it easier for teachers to know which coding lessons fit each state standard, then easier to sell the platform.

Interviews

William and Chance had been mentors for the DC program for years and chaparoned trips for winners to SF, so they got lots of interaction with students

Talked to teachers and uncovered frustrations they had with teaching a coding class as well as frustrationwith the current DC program.

Design

Moved from sketch to wireframe to mocks while testing the entire time.

Brand and Custom Icons

We'd developed the DC logo in the past, but created new icons for each course.

Build

We had to get the new platform ready for the next school year. We knew creating all the content was a huge task. We modified an off-the-shelf content management system to allow staff to start adding in courses in just two weeks.

With the lesson creator app built in record time, we turned our attention to building the platform in React.js to load fast and give best experience to students.

Benefits

98% reduction in support requests
We had to reset students' passwords manually when they would forget them and on-boarding was a nightmare, easily would have over 100 help desk tickets throughout the year, I think this year we've only had a couple platform related questions once we worked out the initial kinks. And none regarding passwords!

Saved hundreds of hours in managing the lessons
Before the rebuild, I basically had to rebuild the lessons every year, which would take up half of my summers. So having a product that we own and control has saved hundreds of hours.

Saved each teacher dozens of hours
Another thing that might be good to mention is the teacher's time. They used to have to get all the students to show them their scores when they finished a quiz, now they are automatically saved into a grade book. So it has saved our clients dozens of hours also. Our teachers have never been happier with the program, they are much more enthusiastic with the program.
